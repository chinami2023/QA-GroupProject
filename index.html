In Memory of
Akira Toriyama

This is in memory of the creator of the hit series Dragon Ball Z, Akira Toriyaka. The purpose of this site is to honour him and his work throughout his life. Please be respectful when posting comments.

Akira Toriyama, a Japanese manga artist and character designer, became famous for his creations like "Dr. Slump" and "Dragon Ball." He also designed characters for popular video games such as "Dragon Quest," "Chrono Trigger," and "Blue Dragon." His work, especially "Dragon Ball," has had a huge impact on manga and inspired many other artists. Toriyama is considered one of the most significant figures in manga history.
